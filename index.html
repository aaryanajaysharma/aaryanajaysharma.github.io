About Me

I am a B.Tech. (Hons.) and MS by Research student in Computer Science and Engineering at the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT-H). My research is focused on the security and privacy aspects of machine learning.

Currently, I am a researcher at the Center for Security, Theory & Algorithmic Research (CSTAR) and SyPy Group, working with Prof. Ankit Gangwal.

I was briefly a Research Intern at Infosys, where I explored red teaming of LLMs and privacy implications of model merging. I will be joining ServiceNow as an AI Researcher, focusing on robust, safe, and context-aware Agentic AI systems.
