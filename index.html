About Me

I am a B.Tech. (Hons.) and MS by Research student in Computer Science and Engineering at the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT-H). My research is focused on the security and privacy aspects of machine learning.

Currently, I am a researcher at the Center for Security, Theory & Algorithmic Research (CSTAR) and SyPy Group, where I work with Prof. Ankit Gangwal on ownership verification and watermarking of Deep Neural Networks (DNNs) and Graph Neural Networks (GNNs). I am also a Research Intern at Infosys, exploring red teaming of LLMs and the privacy implications of model merging.
